The Giving Tree

The Giving Tree is written by Shel Silverstein. Its a story about a tree and a kid that grow up together. The tree loves the boy and gives him everything she has throughout his life. As the boy grows older, he keeps coming back to the tree whenever he needs something. The tree gives him her apples, her branches, and eventually her trunk so he can be happy. In the end, all she has left is a stump, but she is still happy to give the boy a place to sit and rest. The story teaches lessons about love, generosity, and selflessness.
